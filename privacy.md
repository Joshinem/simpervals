Privacy Policy for Simpervals

Effective date: April 2026

Simpervals respects your privacy.

Overview

Simpervals is designed to be a simple interval timer. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect personal data through the app.

Information We Collect

Simpervals does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

We do not collect:

your name
your email address
your location
your health data
your contacts
your photos
your payment details
your usage history on our own servers
Accounts

Simpervals does not require account creation or sign-in.

Internet Access

Simpervals is designed to work without an internet connection for normal use.

Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you choose to save, such as timer values or app preferences, are stored locally on your device only.

This data is not sent to us.

Advertising

Simpervals does not show ads.

Analytics

Simpervals does not use third-party analytics to track you.

Third-Party Services

Simpervals does not use third-party services for app functionality that collect personal data from you through the app.

Please note that Apple may collect certain information as part of providing the App Store, device services, crash reporting, or platform-level analytics. That data is governed by Apple’s own policies, not this app.

Children’s Privacy

Simpervals does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any users.

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect your personal data, we do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone.

Security

Since Simpervals does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers, your information remains on your device for normal app use.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact
